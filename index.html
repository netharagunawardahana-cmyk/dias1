About Me

I’m Nethara Dilmin Dias Gunawardhana, a 19-year-old web designer based in Avissawella, Sri Lanka. I specialize in crafting fast, beautiful, and user-friendly websites that help small businesses build their digital presence and grow.

With a UK Level 3 Diploma in IT from ESOFT, along with a Diploma in English, I bring both technical expertise and strong communication skills to every project. My core focus is UI/UX design and front-end development, ensuring that each website is not only visually engaging but also optimized for performance and accessibility.

Beyond web design, I also have hands-on experience in iPhone customization, UI improvements, and hardware troubleshooting, giving me a unique edge in providing complete digital and technical solutions.

I believe in blending creativity and functionality to deliver digital experiences that stand out. My mission is simple: to turn ideas into impactful designs that connect with people and create real value for businesses.
